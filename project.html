MANIFESTO PROJECT


Wavelengths

My manifesto is an unseen connection that reveals itself in feeling and energy. Wavelengths of human connection are thought of as energy of when good feelings arise with another person in relation to shared communication and understanding, a very verbal description to feeling that gets across without a deep description needed, and an overall feeling of comfort arises in this shared energy. Think about finishing sentences, catching the same humor, knowing exactly what one is describing, and fueling that energy to think. Think about connection and the unexpected experience of connection with a person in touch with sincerity, only to feel as if a wavelength was touched upon to be connected. Some would say we're on the same wavelength, thats what this project will explore: the interaction of shared wavelength energy at first meeting and connection. I would visualize this without words, and hyper-evolve the feeling in a visual take, collaging humans and their signal antenna, signaling waves of connection, and using the energy color purple to represent a complete connection. Overall to consider the wavelengths between people and to follow the signs.
